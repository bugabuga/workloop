Workloop has created an environment where its workforce thrives. We care deeply about our employees and provide work that challenges and excites on a daily basis. If you are looking to work with great people and looking for a long term career path, come on in!

Employee ownership

This is the cherry on top! If you’re looking for a long-term position, you can rest assured your investment in the company is well-rewarded. Workloop’s is unique because we are employee-owned. This means that you have the potential to become a shareholder in Workloop and see a financial return in annual profit shares.

Empowered and respected

We empower our employees so they have a voice all the way through the company up to the board room. Your well-being is core to our company and we make sure you feel the respect for your hard work.

Investing in you

Achieve your short-term and long-term goals! We want to know about you and what your long-term aspirations are. Where we can, we will provide training and guidance to help you achieve them, even if they are 100% unrelated to work.

Benefits/Perks

As well as a choice between generous full time or hourly employment, you will be energized in the office in part due to the ongoing benefits Workloop offers. From healthcare to holidays to other “welcare” perks, we make you feel looked after in return for all the hard work and dedication that you show Workloop.
